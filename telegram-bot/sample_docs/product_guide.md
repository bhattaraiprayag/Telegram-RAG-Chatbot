# Product Guide: TechAssist Pro

Complete user guide for TechAssist Pro - Your AI-Powered Customer Service Platform

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

Before installing TechAssist Pro, ensure your environment meets these requirements:

- **Browser**: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, or Edge 90+
- **Internet**: Minimum 10 Mbps connection recommended
- **Screen**: 1280x720 minimum resolution
- **Integrations**: Access to your CRM and helpdesk systems

### Initial Setup

Follow these steps to set up your TechAssist Pro account:

1. **Create your workspace** - Visit app.techassist.com and click "Start Free Trial"
2. **Configure your brand** - Upload your logo, set colors, and customize the chat widget
3. **Connect data sources** - Import your existing FAQ database and knowledge base
4. **Train the AI** - The system will analyze your data and learn your support patterns
5. **Deploy the widget** - Add our code snippet to your website

Initial setup typically takes 30-60 minutes for basic configuration; complex integrations may require additional time.

## Core Features

### AI Chat Assistant

The AI Chat Assistant is the heart of TechAssist Pro. It:

- Understands natural language queries in 40+ languages
- Learns from every interaction to improve over time
- Handles multiple conversations simultaneously
- Seamlessly escalates to human agents when needed

**Best Practices:**
- Keep your knowledge base updated regularly
- Review AI responses weekly to improve accuracy
- Use custom prompts for industry-specific terminology

### Ticket Management

Our intelligent ticketing system automatically:

- Categorizes incoming tickets by topic and urgency
- Routes tickets to the most qualified agent
- Suggests relevant knowledge base articles
- Tracks resolution time and customer satisfaction

### Analytics Dashboard

Monitor your support performance with real-time analytics:

- **Response Time**: Average first response and resolution times
- **Customer Satisfaction**: CSAT scores and NPS tracking
- **AI Performance**: Accuracy rates and escalation patterns
- **Agent Productivity**: Tickets handled, response quality

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Chat widget not appearing:**
1. Verify the installation code is placed before the closing `</body>` tag
2. Check for JavaScript errors in the browser console
3. Ensure your domain is whitelisted in Settings > Domains

**AI responses are inaccurate:**
1. Review and update your knowledge base content
2. Add more training examples for common questions
3. Use the feedback feature to flag incorrect responses

**Integration not syncing:**
1. Verify API credentials are correct and not expired
2. Check webhook URLs are accessible from internet
3. Review integration logs in Settings > Integrations > Logs
